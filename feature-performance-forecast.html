Performance Forecasting

Know where your campaigns are heading before month-end — and act while there is still time to change the outcome.

Get a Demo

Waiting Until Month-End Is Too Late

A budget trajectory problem visible on day 10 can still be corrected by day 15. The same problem identified on day 28 can only be documented — not fixed. Shirofune's Performance Forecasting gives teams the lead time they need to intervene.

What Shirofune Forecasts

Based on current spend velocity, bid levels, and historical performance patterns, Shirofune generates forward-looking projections for ROAS trajectory, spend trajectory, conversion volume, and LTV performance — updating forecasts daily as new data arrives.

How Forecasting Informs Mid-Month Decisions

When Shirofune surfaces a projected shortfall in ROAS by day 12, the team can review underperforming campaign segments, reallocate budget toward higher performers, and adjust before the gap widens — rather than scrambling at month-end.
