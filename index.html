Product Snapshot

AI Auto-Budgeting

Leaving the optimization of individual campaigns to each ad platform such as Google or Meta, Shirofune maximizes the overall performance across different ad platforms and different campaigns — just like you would increase spend on more performing ad platforms than less performing ones.

Limited Data Learning

The platforms' optimization only works when there is enough data like conversions, and even in such cases, Shirofune's anthropomorphic algorithm works and gets the most possible outcomes with no or limited data.

Smart AI Suggestions

Shirofune makes suggestions only beneficial to advertisers so they can maximize the return. Unlike ad platform suggestions, we never recommend increasing spend unnecessarily.

Budget Controls

Shirofune automatically controls ad spend by setting a budget per ad type, ad platform or budget group in a month or any given period so you never spend too much or too little against your budgets. You can also set MaxCPA/MinROAS instead, so Shirofune automates bidding and budgeting within the set CPA/ROAS.

Deep Analysis

Creating a performance report takes a lot of time and effort, especially when a 3rd-party measurement/attribution tool is involved. Shirofune automatically populates an integrated performance report with a click of a button. It also explains, in natural language, what went wrong and what went well without having to reference all the different data points.

1st/3rd Party Data

By integrating third-party measurement and attribution data from platforms like Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, or Triple Whale with first-party LTV and conversion data from Salesforce, Shopify, or your own database, Shirofune visualizes true advertising effectiveness across channels and ads — so you can optimize based on real outcomes.
