Meet Our Founder

Built on Real Ad Operations Experience

Mitsunaga Kikuchi

Founder & CEO, Shirofune

Mitsunaga Kikuchi founded Shirofune with a clear mission: to eliminate the inefficiencies that keep advertisers and agencies from reaching their full potential.

Before launching Shirofune, Kikuchi built his career at one of Japan's top digital advertising agencies, where he was deeply involved in managing complex multi-platform campaigns. He saw firsthand how even highly skilled teams were forced to spend countless hours on repetitive manual tasks such as adjusting bids, reviewing search terms, pacing budgets, and building reports.

But he also saw that existing automation was not enough. Even with the latest AI and machine learning, most solutions could not match the quality of the day-to-day judgment and execution of experienced ad managers. They could automate pieces of the workflow, but not the real operational expertise that drives performance.

He believed there had to be a better way.

So rather than building just another bidding tool, Kikuchi took a far more ambitious approach: spending more than 20,000 hours systematically translating the judgment, logic, and execution of expert ad managers into technology. By recreating real operational know-how decision by decision, Shirofune was built to achieve a level of automation and performance that conventional algorithms alone could not deliver.

The result was Shirofune, a platform designed to automate execution while preserving strategic control.

Today, Shirofune supports 10+ advertising platforms globally and empowers agencies and brands to scale performance without increasing operational complexity.

"Technology should enhance human expertise, not replace it."

Built by Ad Operations Experts

Shirofune was built by a team of advertising professionals and engineers who have spent over 20,000 hours systematizing the know-how of expert ad managers into an intelligent platform. Our founding team includes veterans from Japan's largest digital advertising agencies and leading technology companies, united by a shared belief that great ad management should not be limited to those with the biggest teams or deepest pockets.

Today, Shirofune serves advertisers and agencies across the United States, Australia, and Japan, helping teams of all sizes deliver enterprise-grade results with far less manual effort.
