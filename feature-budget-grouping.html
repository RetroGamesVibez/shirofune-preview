Manage ad spend across campaigns, channels, and goals as a coordinated portfolio — not as isolated line items.

Get a Demo

The Problem with Campaign-Level Budget Management

Campaign-level budgets create silos where high-performing campaigns are arbitrarily constrained while lower-performing campaigns continue spending simply because they have available budget.

What Budget Grouping Enables

Shirofune's Budget Grouping lets advertisers define pools of budget that can be allocated dynamically across multiple campaigns — by channel, by goal type, or by product line and market.

Real-World Scenario: Acquisition vs. Retention Balancing

With Shirofune Budget Grouping, a combined acquisition budget pool covers all new-customer-focused campaigns, and Shirofune monitors performance daily and redistributes allocation toward the best-performing acquisition campaigns.
