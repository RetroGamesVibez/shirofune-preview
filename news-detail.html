June 24, 2026 Product Update

Shirofune adds native TikTok Ads integration

This is a template — individual articles are managed as WordPress posts/custom-post-types in the CMS build. The content below is a representative example.

Shirofune today announced native support for TikTok Ads, giving advertisers a single place to automate budgeting, bidding, and reporting across TikTok alongside Google, Meta, and Amazon. The addition extends Shirofune's cross-channel automation to one of the fastest-growing platforms in performance marketing.

One workspace for every channel

With the new integration, teams can connect their TikTok Ads accounts in a few clicks and let Shirofune's human-like algorithm manage daily budgets and target CPA/ROAS the same way it does for other platforms. Spend is automatically paced against monthly targets, so campaigns never overshoot budget on high-volume days.

Because TikTok data flows into the same reporting layer as the rest of a customer's stack, marketers get a unified, cross-channel view of performance without stitching together exports from multiple dashboards.

What you can do today

  • Connect one or more TikTok Ads accounts to any Shirofune workspace.
  • Enable AI auto-budgeting with MinROAS or MaxCPA targets per budget group.
  • Receive smart recommendations that highlight opportunities to cut wasted spend.
  • Include TikTok metrics in one-click, cross-channel performance reports.

Our customers asked for TikTok in the same automated workflow they already trust for search and social. Now it's all in one place — with the same guardrails on spend.

Availability

The TikTok Ads integration is available now to all Shirofune customers at no additional cost. To see it in action, request a demo or sign in to connect your account.
