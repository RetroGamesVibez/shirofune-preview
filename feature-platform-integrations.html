Built for the Entire Digital Advertising Ecosystem

Shirofune was built from the ground up for multi-platform advertisers. Every major ad platform is treated as a first-class citizen: bid logic, budget pacing, reporting, and optimization all work consistently across Google Search, Amazon Sponsored Products, or LinkedIn Lead Gen campaigns.

Signal Aggregation Across Channels

Shirofune aggregates signals across all channels to build a more complete picture of campaign performance than any single platform can provide — bid decisions are informed by total portfolio performance, not by a single platform's narrow view.

Cross-Channel Consistency in Optimization

When you set a ROAS target or LTV goal in Shirofune, it applies consistently across every integrated platform. Bid adjustments, budget pacing, performance reporting, and change analysis all function identically regardless of which platform the campaign is running on.
