First-Time Customer Bidding

Shift ad investment toward new customer acquisition — not just repeat buyers — with bidding logic built for new-to-brand growth.

Get a Demo

Why Retargeting-Heavy ROAS Is Misleading

Standard ROAS reporting does not distinguish between orders from new customers and orders from existing repeat buyers. Optimizing for blended ROAS systematically overweights retargeting and underweights the campaigns that actually expand the customer base.

Bidding Specifically on New-to-Brand Customers

Shirofune enables bidding logic that targets new-to-brand customers as a distinct optimization goal, identifying whether a converting user is a first-time customer or a repeat buyer and applying different bid values accordingly.

Strategic Control for eCommerce and Amazon Advertisers

This capability is especially impactful for Amazon advertisers, where the platform's own optimization tends to favor existing customer touchpoints. Combined with Amazon Marketing Cloud data, advertisers can measure new-to-brand purchase rates and shift budget toward acquisition.

Connection to Lifetime ROAS

First-Time Customer Bidding is the operational complement to Lifetime ROAS strategy. Together, they create a coherent acquisition strategy: invest more to acquire new customers because the data shows their long-term value justifies it.
