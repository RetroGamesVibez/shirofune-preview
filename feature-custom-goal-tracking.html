Custom Goal Tracking

Optimize campaigns against the goals that reflect your actual business — not the conversion events that ad platforms default to.

Get a Demo

Why Native Platform Tracking Is Insufficient

Native platform conversions only measure what happens inside the platform's tracking window. For most serious advertisers, last-click attribution overstates certain campaigns, conversion windows don't capture long-cycle deals, and multiple platforms claim the same conversion.

What Shirofune Integrates for Custom Goals

Shirofune connects campaign optimization to third-party attribution systems, custom conversion events, backend revenue events, and LTV signals — ensuring campaigns optimize toward profit, not just conversion volume.

How Custom Goals Improve Optimization Accuracy

When Shirofune optimizes based on your custom goals, campaigns that generate high-value conversions receive more aggressive bidding while low-value conversions receive less budget — tightening the feedback loop between media spend and actual business outcomes.
