Lowering CPA and Lifting Lead Quality by Optimizing to Real Sales Outcomes

Just Right Reader · Education · United States

EducationGoogle AdsMeta Ads
63.07%

114.37%

Conversion volume increase

Just Right Reader partnered with Shirofune to lower their target cost per acquisition (CPA) while enhancing the quality of incoming leads. By integrating HubSpot into their paid media strategies, they were able to focus on actual business outcomes rather than merely optimizing for form fills from within ad platforms.

Challenge
  • Managing bids across multiple platforms proved to be complicated and time-consuming.
  • Traditional optimization methods focused solely on lead volume, neglecting the quality of leads.
  • There was inefficient budget allocation resulting in wasted spending on low-quality submissions.
  • Lack of alignment between paid media performance and actual sales outcomes created challenges in demonstrating ROI.
Solution
  • Developed a cross-channel budget and bid optimization strategy that leveraged data from HubSpot.
  • Shifted optimization criteria from form fills to actual Opportunities in HubSpot, ensuring focus on high-intent leads.
  • Employed continuous refining of bidding practices using Opportunity-level data to maximize budget efficiency.
  • Integrated real-time performance reporting to measure the actual impact on sales pipeline growth.
Results
  • Achieved a 63.07% decrease in cost per acquisition (CPA).
  • Increased conversion volume by 114.37% as targeted towards qualified opportunities.
  • Enhanced overall HubSpot opportunities, supporting significant pipeline growth.
  • Converted paid media from a basic lead generation tool into a strategic revenue driver for the business.

About Just Right Reader

Just Right Reader specializes in providing educational resources tailored to individualized learning needs. Their mission is to empower students and educators with tools that promote effective reading and comprehension skills.
